Aw, dang. Looks like your browser doesn't support WebGL. That's okay: Chrome and Firefox are pretty dang good at it!

The orange lights represent electrons - each one pushing away from one another, but unable to leave the sphere. As they push away and are pushed upon, they accumulate their own velocity, creating a neat show as the electrons interact with one another. At certain times, and with a certain amount of electrons that have successfully spaced themselves evenly from one another, they will form a platonic solid. You can see what shape is being formed by pressing the "connect" button. If you're curious, download the source code on Github.
